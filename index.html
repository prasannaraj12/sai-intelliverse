About the Event

Sai Intelliverse is a multi-disciplinary event designed to bring together bright minds from various fields. We offer a platform for students to showcase their technical expertise and unleash their creative potential through a diverse range of competitions. This year, we are proud to host over 500 participants and 20+ partner organizations, making it our largest event yet. Join us for a journey of learning, competition, and fun!
